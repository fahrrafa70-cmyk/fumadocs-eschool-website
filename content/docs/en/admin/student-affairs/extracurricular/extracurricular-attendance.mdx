Recording attendance is the primary responsibility of an Admin or Coach at every meeting. The **Attendance** page in eSchool is designed so the data entry process becomes very fast. The data you enter here will be automatically calculated into monthly attendance percentages.

## Requirements Before Inputting Attendance

Before you can fill out the attendance sheet, ensure these two things have been met:
1. **Member Registration:** Students must have **Approved** status. Students who are still pending will not appear on the attendance sheet.
2. **Schedule (Timetable):** That extracurricular must already have a meeting schedule in the system.

## Steps for Filling Attendance

<img src="/images/absensi-eskul.png" alt="Add Extracurricular"  />

Follow this procedure at every extracurricular meeting:

### 1. Selecting Meeting Session
- Navigate to **Student Affairs** ‚Üí **Extracurricular** ‚Üí **Input Attendance**.
- **Select Extracurricular:** Use the dropdown to select the activity.
  - *Note:* Coaches will only see the extracurriculars they lead.
- **Select Date:** Click on the calendar picker to determine the practice date. By default, the system will point to today's date.

### 2. Loading Student List
- Click the **Show Students** or **Load Attendance** button.
- The system will display a table of all students who are official members.

### 3. Providing Attendance Status
In the **Action** column, you can select student attendance status using radio buttons. 4 status options are available:

| Option | Explanation |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Present** | Student attended the activity according to schedule. |
| **Sick** | Student was unable to attend due to health reasons. |
| **Permission** | Student was unable to attend with official permission (Leave). |
| **Absent** | Student did not attend without explanation (Alpha). |

#### Quick Action Feature

### 4. Saving Data
- Once all students are marked, scroll to the bottom of the page.
- Click the **Save** button (red floppy disk icon).
- Wait for a success notification from the system.

## Changing Incorrect Attendance

What if you misclicked or just remembered a student had permission after data was saved?

1. Ensure you are on the **Input Attendance** page.
2. Re-select the same **Extracurricular** and **Date** as the data you want to change.
3. Click **Load**. The system will display previously saved data.
4. Change the attendance status of the intended student.
5. Click the **Update Attendance** button.

## Data Validation & Security

- **Schedule Check:** The system will provide a Warning if you try to input attendance on a date that does not match the routine schedule. Even if there is a warning, you can still save it (useful for additional/incidental practices).
- **Change Log:** Every attendance update will be recorded by the system regarding who changed it and when it was done.
- **Cloud Storage:** Data is directly saved to the central server, making it safe from the risks of manual data loss like on paper attendance books.
