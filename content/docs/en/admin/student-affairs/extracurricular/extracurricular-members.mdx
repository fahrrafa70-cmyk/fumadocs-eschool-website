The **Members** page is where Admins and Coaches monitor and manage students who are eligible to participate in activities. This system is designed to handle the registration flow from the student's mobile app until it is approved or rejected by the school.

## Student Registration Flow

In general, the process of becoming a member follows these stages:

1. **Students** select an extracurricular on their phones and click "Register".
2. **Data** enters the Admin dashboard system with **Pending** status.
3. **Admin/Coach** reviews the data.
4. **Decision** is made (Approved/Rejected).
5. **Students** receive instant notifications regarding their registration status.

## Managing Member List

This page is equipped with filter features that are very helpful if your school has thousands of students and dozens of activities.

### 1. Filters & Search
You can narrow down the displayed list based on:
- **Extracurricular Name:** View members of basketball only, for example.
- **Grade Level:** Filter students in grade 10, 11, or 12.
- **Approval Status:** Show only students whose status is still `Pending` for immediate processing.
- **Search Bar:** Type student name or NISN for specific searches.

### 2. Approval Process (Approved)
There are two ways to approve registrations:

#### **A. Quick Approve (Individual)**
- Find a student with Pending status.
- Click the **Checkmark (âœ“)** button in the Action column.
- Click "Yes" on the confirmation.
- Student status changes to **Approved**.

Use this feature if there are many registrants at once at the start of the semester:
- Check the box to the left of student names (or check "Select All" in the header).
- Click the blue **Approve Selected Members** button that appears above the table.

## Member Details (Modal View)

To see complete information before giving approval, click on the student's name row. A detail modal will appear showing:
- **Student Profile Photo**
- **NISN & Grade**
- **Coach Name**
- **Registration Date**

At the bottom of this modal, the **Approve** and **Reject** buttons are also available as alternative navigation.

---

## Exporting Member Reports

Do you need to submit a list of members to the Principal or Education Office?
- Click the **Export** button.
- Select format (e.g., **Excel**).
- The file will be downloaded with an automatic name: `extracurricular-members-DATE.xlsx`.
- Reports include Name, Grade, NISN, and Registration Date.

## Troubleshooting

- **Student Claims to have Registered but Doesn't Appear?**
  1. Ask students to ensure they have an internet connection when pressing the register button on their phones.
  2. Make sure you are not activating the wrong filter (e.g., a different grade filter).
  3. Try pressing the **Refresh** button on the table.

- **Cannot Click the Approve Button?**
  Ensure your account has `extracurricular-edit` permission. Contact the Super Admin if the menu is locked for you.
