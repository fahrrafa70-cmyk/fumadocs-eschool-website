**Add-ons** are optional feature extensions that can be purchased separately on top of your base subscription plan. They allow schools to unlock specific capabilities without upgrading to a higher-tier plan.

## Overview

| Item | Description |
|------|-------------|
| **Purpose** | Purchase optional extra features |
| **Required Role** | School Admin |
| **Route** | `/addons/plan` |

---

## Activating an Add-on

<Steps>
  <Step>
    Navigate to **Subscription → Add-ons** from the sidebar
  </Step>
  <Step>
    Browse the list of available add-ons
  </Step>
  <Step>
    Click **Purchase** on the desired add-on
  </Step>
  <Step>
    Complete the payment through the available payment gateway
  </Step>
  <Step>
    Once payment is confirmed, the add-on feature will be instantly enabled
  </Step>
</Steps>

---

## Types of Add-ons

Add-ons typically extend the capability of specific modules. Examples include:

| Add-on | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **Extra Storage** | Increase document storage capacity |
| **Advanced Reports** | Unlock detailed analytics and exportable reports |
| **SMS Notifications** | Enable SMS alerts for attendance and payments |
| **Additional Users** | Increase the maximum number of active users |

<Callout type="info">
  The exact add-ons available depend on what the Super Admin has configured for your region. Contact your system administrator for the current add-on catalog.
</Callout>

---

## Managing Active Add-ons

Active add-ons are displayed on the Add-ons page with their expiry dates. Some add-ons are one-time purchases, while others may be subscription-based with renewal requirements.
