The **Upload Results** feature allows Teachers to enter student offline exam results into the system directly through the provided score entry interface.

> [!IMPORTANT]
> Entering exam results **can only be done after the exam ends** or on the day following exam execution. Make sure the exam schedule status is marked "Completed" before entering scores.
