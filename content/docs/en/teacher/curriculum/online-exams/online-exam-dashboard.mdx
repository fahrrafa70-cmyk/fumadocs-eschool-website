The Online Exam Dashboard is the main display that presents statistical summaries and real-time monitoring of online exam implementation. This page serves as a control center for Teachers to monitor exam status, student performance, and overall exam activities for their assigned classes.

---

### Online Exam Dashboard Components

**Total Exams**  
Displays the total number of online exams created by the Teacher.

**Active Exams**  
Shows the number of exams currently in progress (live).

**Upcoming Exams**  
Number of exams that have been scheduled but not yet started.

**Completed Exams**  
Number of exams that have been completed.

---

### Participation Statistics

Displays a donut chart illustrating student participation status across all exams managed:
- Not Yet Participated
- In Progress
- Completed

This visualization helps monitor student progress on available exams.

---

### Active Exam List

This section displays details of exams currently in progress, with the following information:
- Exam Title
- Subject
- Class
- Number of Active Participants
- Status (Active/Completed)

---

### Overall Exam Performance

Displayed here are performance metrics of students in taking online exams in the Teacher's classes:
- **Average Score** → Average score across all exams.
- **Pass Rate** → Percentage of students who achieved passing grades.
- **High Performance** → Percentage of students with high scores.
- **Low Performance** → Percentage of students with low scores.

---

### Performance Per Subject

Details exam performance statistics per subject taught:
- Subject Name
- Number of exams conducted.
- Average score for that subject.

This feature facilitates specific performance analysis per field of study.

---

### Upcoming Exams

Displays a list of scheduled exams created by the Teacher that have not yet started:
- Exam Title
- Subject
- Class
- Start Date
- Status (Upcoming)

---

### Recently Completed Exams

This section shows a list of recently completed exams, with details:
- Exam Title
- Subject
- Class
- Number of Participants
- Average Score

---

### Main Functions of Online Exam Dashboard:

- Monitor exam status (active, upcoming, completed).
- Track student participation statistics in real-time.
- View exam performance trends globally and per subject taught.
- Quickly access upcoming exam lists and completed exam results.
