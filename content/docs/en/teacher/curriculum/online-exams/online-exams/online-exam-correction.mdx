The Online Exam Correction feature is used to check and provide assessments for essay answers from students who have taken online exams in your classes. The grading process is done manually by the Teacher by determining whether the answers given by students are **Correct** or **Incorrect**, and providing scores according to question weight.

---

### Access Online Exam Correction Page

1. Go to **Online Exams** → **Online Exams** menu.
2. In the exam list table, click the three-dot icon (⋮) in the **Action** column.
3. Select the **Correction** option.
4. The system will display the **Online Exam Correction** page containing the list of questions from the exam.

---

### General Information on Correction Page

**Class Name**  
The class taking the exam.

**Subject**  
The subject name of the exam.

**Online Exam Title**  
The name of the exam to be graded.

**Total Score**  
Total score weight of all questions in the exam.

---

### View and Grade Student Answers

<img src="/images/daftar-soal-ujian.png" alt="Image description"/>

1. Under **Exam Question List**, click the **View Student Answers** button on the question to be graded.
2. The system will display the **Grade Student Answers** modal.

---

### Grade Student Answers Form (Modal Pop-up)

In this grading modal, you will see:

- **Student Name** → Name of the student being graded.
- **Answer** → Text answer filled in by the student.
- **Grading Options**:
  - ✔ **Correct** → Mark student answer as correct.
  - ✖ **Incorrect** → Mark student answer as incorrect.
- **Score** → Enter score (maximum according to question weight).
- There is also a **MAX** button to directly give full score to that answer.

---

### Saving Grading Results

After selecting answer status and filling in the score, click the **Save Correction** button to save the assessment results.

---

### Functions of This Correction:

- Provide manual assessment for essay-type questions that require subjective assessment.
- Determine whether student answers meet assessment criteria.
- Adjust answer scores flexibly (not automatic).

---

### Brief Student Answer Grading Process:

1. Students take the exam.
2. Teachers access the **Correction** option in the Online Exams menu.
3. Teachers select questions → click **View Student Answers**.
4. Determine answer status (Correct/Incorrect) and give score.
5. Save correction results.
