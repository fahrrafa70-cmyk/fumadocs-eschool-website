The **Student Leave Permits** page is the center for managing and approving school leave applications submitted by students or student guardians in the classes you manage. Through this feature, Teachers can monitor student absence reasons transparently and perform validation through the sent supporting attachments.

---

### Permit List Filter

To find specific data, you can use the following filters:
- **Class**: Select a specific class to narrow down the student list.
- **Date Range**: Specify the **Start Date** and **End Date** to view permit history within a certain period.
- **Search**: Search for student names directly through the search column.

---

### Leave Permit List Table

This table summarizes all permit applications being processed or completed:

| Column | Description |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Name & Class** | Identity of the student applying for leave. |
| **Reason** | Brief summary of the cause for leave (Sick, Family Leave, etc.). |
| **Date** | Period of time the student is unable to attend school. |
| **Status** | Current status (*Pending*, *Approved*, or *Rejected*). |
| **Action** | Quick buttons to process the permit. |

---

### Processing Permit Status

There are two ways for Teachers to give a decision:

**1. Through the Action Column (Quick)**
- Click the **Green Checkmark** icon to immediately provide an **Approved** status.
- Click the **Pink Cross** icon to provide a **Rejected** status.

**2. Through Permit Details (Complete)**
Click on the student's name row to open the detail window. Here you can see:
- **Supporting Attachments**: Photos of medical certificates or letters from guardians. Click the image to enlarge.
- **Full Reason**: Detailed explanation from the applicant.
- **Decision Form**: **Approve** or **Reject** buttons at the bottom.

> [!IMPORTANT]
> If you **Reject** an application, it is highly recommended to fill in the **Rejection Reason** column so that students or guardians get clear information.
