## Coach Workflow

To carry out your duties as a coach effectively, follow this workflow:

1. **Manage Schedule:** Set routine days and meeting times in the [Activity Schedule](./teacher-extracurricular-schedule) menu.
2. **Member Approval:** Check and fulfill student registration requests in the [Member List](./teacher-extracurricular-members) menu.
3. **Record Attendance:** Always fill in the attendance list every time a meeting is finished in the [Input Attendance](./teacher-extracurricular-attendance) menu.
4. **Monitoring:** Evaluate your students' activity in the [Reports & Recap](./teacher-extracurricular-attendance-report) menu.
